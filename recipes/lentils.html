Coconut Ginger Lentils

Description

Another indian-inspired lentil dish.

Ingredients

  • Beluga Lentils
  • Coconut Milk
  • Onion, Ginger
  • Fresh Coriander and Lemon

Steps

  1. Make soffrito with Onionand olive oil
  2. Add lentils, cook for 20 minutes
  3. Add Coconut Milk and ginger, cook for another 10 minutes
  4. Serve with fresh coriander and a squeeze of lemon

Simple Dal

Description

An indian-inspired lentil dish.

Ingredients

  • Red Lentils
  • Coconut Milk
  • Canned Tomatoes
  • Olive Oil, Garlic, Onion, Ginger, Garam Masala, Chili
  • Fresh Coriander and Lemon

Steps

  1. Make soffrito with Garlic, Onion, Ginger, Garam Masala, Chili
  2. Add tomatoes and lentils, cook for 10 minutes
  3. Add Coconut Milk, cook for another 10 minutes
  4. Serve with fresh coriander and a squeeze of lemon
